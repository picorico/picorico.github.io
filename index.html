About

Welcome to the home of the first open source bike telemetry platform.
Learn how to build sensors, collect data and make sense of the adjustments available to you.

Where did the name come from?

Naming things is always tricky and can be the hardest part of a project. We took the quick route and went with some of the first things that came to mind. Thinking that our sensors are tiny and providing a rich amount of data we thought to combine “pico” and “rico”

What is downhill mountain bike racing?

Downhill mountain bike racing is a specialized form of bike racing where riders typically go to ski mountains to race against the clock on a course that includes rough terrain from the top to the bottom.
